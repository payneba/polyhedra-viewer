Polyhedra Data Attribution
==========================

The polyhedra geometry data in this directory (vertex coordinates and face
definitions) originates from:

  "Virtual Polyhedra: The Encyclopedia of Polyhedra"
  by George W. Hart
  http://www.georgehart.com/virtual-polyhedra/vp.html

The data was converted to JSON format by Lee Stemkoski and distributed via
the npm package "polyhedra" by Finn Pauls (https://github.com/finnp/polyhedra).
